How it Works Section

In order to support local businesses and promote volunteer work in the community, Brownie Points develops and operates a point-based rewards program to give back to those giving back in the community. Verified non-profits reward Brownie Points users with brownie points that can be exchanged for deals and discounts with local businesses.
