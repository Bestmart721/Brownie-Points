Welcome to Service Points

Service Points supports local businesses, non-profits, and volunteers by giving back to those who give back.

About Us

The Service Points team is composed of three hard working college students from the Philadelphia area. This project for social good was created by Brandon Wong, a chemical engineering Ph.D.student at Drexel University, Brandon Coons, an electrical engineering student and Drexel University, and Thomas Gerot, a cybersecurity student at Valley Forge Military College.

How it Works

In order to support local businesses and promote volunteer work in the community, Service Points develops and operates a point-based rewards program to give back to those giving back in the community. Verified non-profits reward Service Points users with points that can be exchanged for deals and discounts with local businesses.
